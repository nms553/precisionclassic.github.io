ABOUT - This particular web site is devoted exclusively to the Agco precision replica lines. The Precision Series lines are some of the most highly detailed replicas of farm machinery ever produced. Listed on the main page you will find a brief description of all the current Agco Precision Series releases. You will also find a few special editions and variations. Since these collectors items are not toys you will find the prices may vary greatly depending on several points. The age of the item, the rarity of the item, condition of the replica, the completeness of the replica and the condition of the box. Therefore, inventory may vary in price accordingly. Please keep in mind that the inventory may not necessarily be new. Many items I may purchase second hand because of the length of time that they have been discontinued. Each item is opened for inspection, its condition is carefully evaluated, it is returned to its original packing and notations are made along with each listing. I do my best to make a thorough inspection of each replica and box to provide an accurate description of each item. - Mike Schweissguth.

SHIPPING - All items are carefully packaged to insure safe arrival. Typically each item will be wrapped in plastic and then placed in an oversize box with a minimum of two inches of shredded paper to cushion and protect the replica. All items are shipped fully insured via FedEx unless another carrier is requested. Please provide us with an physical address that will have someone available to sign for the package.
